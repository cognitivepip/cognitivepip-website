FAQ

Do you offer free signals?
Yes — limited setups are shared in the Free Group. For full guidance with precise Entry, SL and TP, VIP is recommended.
Are payments monthly?
No. All education product payments are once-off with lifetime access. No monthly fees or renewals.
What is the difference between the Hub and the courses?
The Hub covers Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced in structured summaries — your foundation. Full courses go deep on each topic. SMC, ICT, CRT, TA and FA are course-only.
Does VIP include the Hub?
No. VIP and the Hub are standalone products. The VIP + Hub Bundle at R1,050 gives you both with a saving of R150.
Can I join VIP for free?
Yes — refer 3 verified VIP members at standard price and your access is free. Contact admin for your referral tracking format.
Is profit guaranteed?
No. Trading involves risk. CognitivePip focuses on building skill, discipline and consistency — not making promises about returns.
I'm a complete beginner, can I join?
Yes. The Educational Hub and the upcoming Beginner Course are designed specifically for people starting from zero.
Do you run specials or discounts?
Yes — a few times per year tied to specific events. All specials are announced publicly with a hard deadline. Follow our channels to never miss one.
